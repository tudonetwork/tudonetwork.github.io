Natal: fun and nature

The 400 kilometers of coast of Natal and surroundings, framed by dunes, reefs, cliffs and natural pools, make the capital of Rio Grande do Norte one of the most sought after destinations in the country.

Natal, the capital of the sun!

Natal is the capital of the state of Rio Grande do Norte, in the northeast end of Brazil. It is known for its extensive coastal sand dunes and the star-shaped Reis Magos Fort, a 16th-century Portuguese fortress at the mouth of the Potengi River.

Pipa Beach

The beaches of Pipa, are paradisiacal beaches, calm, with waves, with many waves and wind, good gastronomy, aquatic activities on the beach with dolphins.

A lot of fun

In Natal various activities is involved - in addition to the up and down the dunes, offers river crossing with raft, sea bath and lagoon bath. Paying separately, you can visit Aquarius, ride in a donkey and dromedary, do squib (sandboard sitting) and aerobunda (zip line).
